Name Description
 Competitor : Influencer Category

A rival enterprise in a struggle for advantage over the subject enterprise.

 Customer : Influencer Category

A role played by an individual or enterprise that has investigated, ordered, received, or paid for products or services from the subject enterprise.

 Environment : Influencer Category

The aggregate of surrounding conditions or Influencers affecting the existence or development of an enterprise.

 Partner : Influencer Category

An enterprise that shares risks and profit with the subject enterprise (or is associated with the subject enterprise to share risks and profit) because this is mutually beneficial.

 Regulation : Influencer Category

An order prescribed by an authority such as a government body or the management of an enterprise.

 Supplier : Influencer Category

A role played by an individual or enterprise that can furnish or provide products or services to the subject enterprise.

 Technology : Influencer Category

The role of technology, including its developments and limitations — there may be prerequisites for use of technology; there may be enterprise activity that technology enables or restricts.

 Assumption : Influencer Category

Something that is taken for granted or without proof.

 Corporate : Influencer Category

Value An ideal, custom, or institution that an enterprise promotes or agrees with (either positive or negative).

 Explicit Value : Influencer Category

A Corporate Value explicitly set forth and declared.

 Implicit Value : Influencer Category

A Corporate Value not explicitly declared but nonetheless understood by some or all of the people in an enterprise.

 Habit : Influencer Category

A customary practice or use.

 Infrastructure : Influencer Category

The basic underlying framework or features of a system.

 Issue : Influencer Category

A point in question or a matter that is in dispute as between contending partners.

 Management : Influencer Category

Prerogative A right or privilege exercised by virtue of ownership or position in an enterprise.

 Resource : Influencer Category

The resources available for carrying out the business of an enterprise, especially their quality.
